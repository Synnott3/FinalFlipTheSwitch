Background Programs

Solution

One of the most common reasons for a slow computer is programs running in the background. Close any unnecessary background programs to improve the performance of your computer.

Clean Hard Drive

Solution

As a best practice, perform a disk cleanup at least once a month. This will delete temporary files, empty the Recycle Bin, and remove various files and other items that are no longer needed. By reducing the amount of unnecessary and temporary files on your hard drive your computer will run faster.
